IIIT-BH WINTER OF CODE
About

IIIT-BH Winter of Code is an initiative by P-SOCIETY, IIIT Bhubaneswar for the students, who have never participated in Free or Open Source Software (FOSS) development before, to get them involved during the winter vacations and break the ice of Open Source contributions. Throughout the program, participants contribute to different projects under guidance of experienced mentors.

How it works?

Participants can apply for the program either as a student or as a mentor. Mentors are selected through a selection process. The selected mentors propose projects, the best of which are chosen to be included in the program. Students work on these projects in their winter vacations. The mentors review the pull requests and the overall work on their project by various contributors throughout the coding period of the program. At the end of the coding period, the students are compared on the basis of most contributions.

Why IIIT-BH WOC?

Intro to open source

WoC provides a great opportunity to get acquainted with Github along with Git commands and contribute to open source effeciently.

Brush up your coding skills

If you possess a passion for coding and software development, then WoC is just the right platform.

Learn by collaborating

With WoC, learn about selecting a project, interacting with mentors and working together towards a common goal.
